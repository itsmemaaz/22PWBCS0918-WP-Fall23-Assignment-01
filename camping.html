CAMPING   IN   GALYAT :


Camping in Galyat is super fun! You get to sleep in a tent under the open sky, surrounded by big mountains. There are cool spots to set up your camp, maybe near a bubbling stream or in a quiet forest.



At night, you can make a campfire, tell stories, and gaze at the stars. It's like a big outdoor sleepover with the sounds of nature all around you. In the morning, you wake up to the awesome views of the Galyat mountains, making it a camping adventure you won't forget!
