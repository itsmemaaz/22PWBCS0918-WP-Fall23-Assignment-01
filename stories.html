Welcome To Explore The Beauty Of Galyat!


Galyat :


Galyat is in the Murree region of the Rawalpindi District in Punjab, Pakistan. These hill stations are known for their natural beauty, pleasant weather, and lush landscapes. Here are some aspects of the beauty you might explore:



Location:


Galyat is in the Murree region of the Rawalpindi District in Punjab, Pakistan. it is surrounded by the beautiful Karakoram mountains. It's like a cozy spot in Murree with green hills, perfect for a peaceful getaway.

Scenic Beauty:


Galyat is renowned for snow-capped mountains, clear lakes, and lush valleys. The charm of Nathia Gali, Mushkpuri, and other attractions make it a picturesque haven for nature lovers..



Trekking and Mountaineering:


Galyat is great for hiking and climbing. The hills are perfect for adventures, and it's a starting point to reach really tall mountains.
